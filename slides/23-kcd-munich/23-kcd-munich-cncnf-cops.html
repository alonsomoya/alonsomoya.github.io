A shift in roles

Ted meets scrum

Communities of Practice

Group of people that share a passion or concern for what they do and interact regularly to make it better

- For those of you new to this - It's a term conined already in the 90s by Wegner Taylor - And they represents a Group of people that share a passion or concern for what they do and interact regularly to make it better - Recently it has become very popular in the enterprise given the popularity of agile methodologies and multi-disciplinary teams - As we indeed share a common passion for cloud technologies in this conference, we would just need to meet regularly to be called community of practice
